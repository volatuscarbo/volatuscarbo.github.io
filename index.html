VolatusCarbo Aviation Auditing & Consultancy

Aviation Climate Compliance • Auditing • Regulatory Advisory

Volatus Carbo Aviation Auditing & Consultancy LTD is headquartered in Paphos, Cyprus, conveniently located near Paphos International Airport (LCPH). The company operates with a flexible team of experienced freelance consultants, each bringing extensive expertise in aviation emissions compliance, and EU ETS auditing.

Volatus Carbo Aviation Auditing & Consultancy LTD was established as an offspring initiative of ETSVerification, with the specific purpose of supporting airlines beyond the scope of statutory verification activities. The company was created to provide operators with continuous compliance support, regulatory advisory services, and operational guidance, complementing—but not replacing—the formal verification process.
